Privacy Policy

kkemii (hereinafter referred to as the 'Company') establishes and discloses this Privacy Policy to protect the personal information of users using the QuitMate service (hereinafter referred to as the 'Service') and to handle related grievances promptly and smoothly.

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The 'Company' processes personal information for the following purposes. The personal information being processed will not be used for purposes other than the following, and if the purpose of use changes, necessary measures such as obtaining separate consent will be taken.

  • Service provision and management: Smoking cessation statistics calculation, smoking cessation journal recording, milestone achievement confirmation, etc.
  • User identification: Identity verification and customized service provision according to service use

2. Items of Personal Information Processed

The 'Company' collects only the minimum amount of information for smooth service provision, and most data is stored locally on the user's device.

  • Required items: Service usage data such as nickname, smoking cessation start date, daily smoking amount, cigarette price, etc.
  • Automatically collected items: Device information (model name, OS version), app usage logs (for system error analysis via Sentry and service usage pattern analysis via Google Firebase Analytics)

3. Processing and Retention Period of Personal Information

In principle, the 'Company' destroys the relevant information without delay once the purpose of collecting and using personal information has been achieved. However, if a user executes 'Reset Data' within the app or deletes the app, local data is immediately deleted.

4. Third-Party Advertising and Data Collection (Google AdMob)

QuitMate uses the 'Google AdMob' advertising platform within the app to provide the service free of charge to users. To provide customized ads (targeted ads) and measure ad performance, Google and its partners may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (e.g., iOS IDFA), cookies, device information, and app usage data in an anonymized state.

  • Purpose of Collection: Provision of customized ads and performance analysis
  • Collected Items: Advertising identifiers, app usage history, device information
  • Recipient of Information: Google LLC

Users can refuse customized ad tracking (ATT) at any time through their device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking). Even if refused, basic ads will be shown, but personalized ads will not be provided.

For more information on Google's data collection and processing, please refer to the link below.
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Rights and Obligations of Users and How to Exercise Them

Users can view, modify, or delete (Reset Data) their personal information at any time through the in-app settings.

6. Privacy Officer

The Company has designated a Privacy Officer as follows to take overall responsibility for personal information processing tasks and handle user complaints and damage relief.

  • Contact: support@kkemii.com
